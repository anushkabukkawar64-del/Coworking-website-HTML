Coworking Solutions

Flexible workspace for individuals and small teams

Join a vibrant community of professionals

Coworking at WORKSPACE combines the best of both worlds: the flexibility of working independently with the energy and networking opportunities of a shared environment.

Hot Desk - $299/month

Work from any available desk in the coworking area. Perfect for those who value variety and want to network with different professionals each day.

  • Access during business hours (7am-7pm)
  • First-come, first-served seating
  • 5 meeting room hours per month
  • Community events included

Dedicated Desk - $499/month

Your own desk in a shared coworking area. Keep your setup, personalize your space, and build relationships with desk neighbors.
