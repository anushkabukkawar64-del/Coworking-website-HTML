FAQ

Membership Questions

Common questions about WORKSPACE memberships

What's the commitment term?

We offer month-to-month memberships with no long-term commitment required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We also offer 6-month and annual contracts with discounted rates (10% and 20% off respectively).

Can I try before I commit?

Absolutely! Schedule a tour at any location to see the space and meet the community. We also offer day passes ($35) so you can experience WORKSPACE firsthand before becoming a member.

How do meeting room bookings work?

Meeting rooms can be booked through our mobile app or member portal. Reservations are available up to 30 days in advance. Your monthly allocation of meeting hours rolls over for one month if unused. Additional hours can be purchased at $25/hour.

What happens if I need to upgrade my membership?

You can upgrade your membership at any time. The change takes effect immediately or on your preferred date. If upgrading mid-month, we'll prorate the difference. Many members start with Hot Desk and upgrade to Dedicated Desk or Private Office as their needs grow.

Is there a discount for annual memberships?

Yes! Annual memberships receive a 20% discount on monthly rates, and 6-month commitments receive 10% off. You'll also receive priority access to new locations and exclusive member benefits. Contact us to discuss annual membership options.

Do you offer team or corporate pricing?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for teams of 10 or more members. Enterprise solutions with custom pricing are available for large organizations. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and receive a customized quote.
