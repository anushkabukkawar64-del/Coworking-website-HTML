Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WORKSPACE

What's included in my membership?

All WORKSPACE memberships include 24/7 access to your workspace, high-speed WiFi, unlimited printing, access to meeting rooms (based on availability), complimentary beverages including coffee and tea, access to communal areas, mail handling services, and invitations to exclusive member events and networking opportunities.

Can I access multiple locations?

Yes! With our All Access membership, you can work from any WORKSPACE location worldwide. This gives you the flexibility to work wherever your business takes you, whether that's across town or across the globe.

What are the contract terms?

We offer flexible membership terms to suit your needs. Options include month-to-month memberships with no long-term commitment, as well as 6-month and annual contracts with discounted rates. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your membership with 30 days' notice.

Is there parking available?

Parking availability varies by location. Many of our buildings offer dedicated parking for members at an additional monthly fee. We also provide bike storage at most locations and are typically located near public transportation for easy access.

Can I bring guests?

Absolutely! Members can bring guests to their workspace. For meeting rooms, you can book and host client meetings or team gatherings. We also offer day passes if you have colleagues visiting who need workspace for the day.

What if my team grows?

That's great news! WORKSPACE is designed to scale with your business. You can easily upgrade from a hot desk to a dedicated desk, or from a small office to a larger one. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition as your needs evolve.
